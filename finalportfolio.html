About Me

Hi! My name is Yanmin and I am undergraduate currently attending the University of California San Diego! The story of how I decided to pursue UX/UI design is quite funny. Coming into college, I did't know what I wanted to do. I bounced around, from being undeclared to studying International Business. It wasn't until my sophomore year of college when I decided to going into design. I was playing basketball with a couple people and played against a PhD Cognitive Science student. He told me a little about the major and what it entailed. When I decided to check it out, I found the material fun and engaging which led me to pursue Cognitive Science!

Projects

Here are some of the things I've worked on!

Amazon Sustainability Redesign

Roles: Designer & Researcher

A redesign of the Amazon website to include a dedicated page for users to buy sustainable goods on Amazon and show how those products are sustainable.

Reddit App Redesign

Roles: Designer and Researcher

A redesign of the Reddit mobile app that adds a feature to provide context for posts that may include misleading information in hopes of fighting misinformation.

Travel Buddy Startup

Roles: Researcher, Video Production, Kickstarter Setup

A project where we conceptualized and created a mock startup for a travel app that allows users to plan and find people to travel with.

UCSD App Redesign

Roles: Designer and Researcher

A redesign of the UCSD mobile app to help streamline the user interface and reduce the amount of scrolling needed. The redesign also focuses on highlighting the most useful features of the UCSD app and making those features more accessible.
